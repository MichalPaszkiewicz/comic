The Science of Motivation

When gravity pulls you down...
you just need a bit more air resistance.
If you are feeling dark, remember...
even black holes give out some radiation.
If a butterfly's wings can affect the weather...
you can do so much more just by getting out of bed.
If you can't do it at t...
do it at t + 1.
If you don't take the limit of your function to ∞ ...
You might not converge on the goal you want.
